Dr. Theodor Rosebury discusses his book "Life on Man"

BROADCAST: 1969 | DURATION: 00:57:40

Synopsis

Dr. Theodor Rosebury dissects cultural ideas around cleanliness and shame as presented in his book "Life of Man".
